OUR VALUES/POLICY

  • Safety

    We shall strive to maintain high safety standards which are critical in delivering high quality health care.

  • Caring with compassion

    We shall provide loving care in an enviroment that replocates the warmth & comfort of home.

  • Quality

    We shall strive to maintain high standard by recruiting, retaining & updating excellent staff that delivers excellent family centred health care.

  • Team work

    We will strive to create unified integrated approach.

  • Education & Social responsibilitu

    We shall provide educational programs for health awareness to our clients and community at large

  • Work enviroment

    We are commited to give value, respect & an oppurtunity for personal and professional growth.

  • Enviromental responsibility

    We shall strive to be an eco friendly hospital by using renewable resources in terms of energy, water used and proper biomedical waste disposable.

  • Financial commitment

    The hospiter is commited to financial stability through efficient services and competitive pricing.
